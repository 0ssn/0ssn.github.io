Our Timeline Highlights

2017/2018

National Omani day

This event was held in the city called Vlissingen as a lot of foreigners and Omani gathered on that day to celebrate the National day together.

2018/2019

Wajha

In this year,  an activity called Wajha was held, in this activity all the study information and what you need to know about NL was discussed and a booklet was created.

2019/2020

competition Year

Numerous types of contests were held in this year and big prizes were announced for the winners as there were 3 types of the contest which are:
Photography, Art and Poetry

2020/2021

Corona year

Of course, this year is when the pandemic started as a lot of the students were studying online and there was not that much going on as everything was closed until the next year which is 2021
